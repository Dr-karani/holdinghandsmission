About Us

Holding Hands Mission Recovery Centre is a rehabilitation centre dedicated to helping individuals overcome drug and alcohol addiction. Located in Mirera along Naivasha–Mai Mahiu Road, between Lake Naivasha and Mt. Longonot, we provide a safe and supportive environment for healing.

Our approach focuses on structure, compassion, and long-term recovery — helping every individual turn their struggles into strengths.
